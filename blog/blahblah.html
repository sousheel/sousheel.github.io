Forgive me for sounding absolutely stupid and crazy, these are only ideas that I must organize sometime. Counter arguments also need to be thought of.

#1 July 20, 2017: Everything we know is created by synthesis. This meay mean synthesizing words to create new meaning (sentences) or synthesizing a horn and a horse to get a unicorn. Because of this, I believe all knowledge is heirarchical. We must synthesize lower level ideas together in order to get to the next level of knowledge.

#2 July 20, 2017: Inference is impossible, because it is impossible to know the state of everything in the world. Therefore, all the inferences we make can be compromised. I believe this makes the world rely on probability.
